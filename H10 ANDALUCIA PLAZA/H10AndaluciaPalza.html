Hotel H10 Andalucia Plaza

The H10 Andalucía Plaza - Adults Only is located in Marbella, just 800 m from Puerto Banús and 500 m from the beach. It features an outdoor swimming pool.

Some rooms at the hotel have views over the swimming pool. The design is stylish, with modern furniture and modern art. All include flat-screen satellite TV and a minibar. Seasonal air conditioned and heating are available.

A free towel service is available at the pool, surrounded by a sun terrace with loungers. The hotel also has a free gym and a spa available for an extra charge, which includes a Turkish bath and sauna.

The Córdoba buffet restaurant opens onto a terrace full of palm trees. Á la carte restaurant is also available at La Brasserie. The Bar Toledo offers live music and Bar Naturalia can be found next to the pool.
